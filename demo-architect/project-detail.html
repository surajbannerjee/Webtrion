EDEN WELLNESS SPA

A serene retreat where modern design blends with nature to restore
balance and calm.

2024

Hospitality Interior Design

Description

Coastal suites blending wood, linens, and ocean tones for a calm experience.

Design Philosophy

This project centers around the idea of “quiet luxury” — a design approach that emphasizes simplicity, natural materials, and thoughtful detail. The goal was to create an environment that feels collected yet effortless, tailored to the Bennetts’ lifestyle of intentional living.

Client’s Lifestyle

The clients, James and Olivia Bennett, are a creative couple working in the fields of digital media and publishing. With a lifestyle that blends global travel, remote work, and a deep appreciation for art and culture, their home needed to serve multiple functions while remaining visually cohesive.

  • They desired a home that feels effortlessly curated, with pieces collected over time rather than overly styled.
  • Functionality was key — the space needed to support remote work, quiet evenings, and occasional entertaining.
  • As frequent travelers, they also wanted to return to a home that felt like a calm, grounding sanctuary after time abroad.
Design Philosophy

The project was guided by a clear design intention: to craft a home that reflects the Bennetts’ modern sensibility, while fostering ease, comfort, and subtle sophistication.

  • A neutral, textural palette was chosen to create a timeless base — allowing furniture and art to stand out without overwhelming the space.
  • Materials were selected with care: natural wood, linen, stone, and handcrafted ceramics contribute warmth and authenticity.
  • The layout was reimagined to optimize flow and natural light, encouraging both focus and relaxation depending on the moment.
  • Every space was designed with purpose — from the sunlit reading corner to the multifunctional kitchen island, each zone responds to real, daily rituals.
  • Thoughtful layering of textures, lighting, and meaningful objects brings the space to life, inviting connection, reflection, and ease.
  • Above all, the project delivers on its promise: a home that doesn’t just look beautiful — it feels intuitively right.
Location Santa Monica,
California
Duration 8 weeks
Date Jul 20, 2023
